How this Works

Loyalty points are rewards you earn every time you make a purchase. The more you buy, the more points you collect — and those points can later be used to get discounts, special offers, or free items.
Every time you place an order, you earn points based on your total bill.

For example:

  • Spend Rs. 100 → Earn 1 point
  • Spend Rs. 1000 → Earn 10 points

(You can set your own point system.)
