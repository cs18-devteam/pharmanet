Generic name: paracetamol

Other brand names of paracetamol include: Panadol, Calpol,Tylenol, Alvedon

Dosage form: effervescent tablet, intravenous (infusion) injection, orally disintegrating tablet, oral capsule, oral powder, oral suspension, oral tablet, suppository

Paracetamol: The Essential Pain Reliever

Paracetamol, also known as acetaminophen, is one of the most commonly used medicines in the world. It is widely trusted for its ability to reduce pain and lower fever, making it a household essential for both adults and children.

What Is Paracetamol?

Paracetamol is an analgesic (pain reliever) and antipyretic (fever reducer). It works by blocking chemical signals in the brain that create the feeling of pain and by regulating the body’s temperature to reduce fever.

Common Uses

  • Headache
  • Fever
  • Toothaches
  • Cold and flu symptoms
  • Muscle or joint pain
  • Menstrual cramps

It is gentle on the stomach and safe for most people when used correctly.

How It Works

Unlike many painkillers, paracetamol does not reduce inflammation. Instead, it targets the central nervous system and blocks the enzymes that send pain signals to the brain. It also acts in the brain’s temperature-control center to help reduce fever.

Dosage & Safety

For adults:

  • 500–1000mg every 4–6 hours
  • Maximum limit: 4000mg/day

For children:

  • Doses depend on weight
  • Follow pharmacist or label instructions

Paracetamol is generally safe, but taking too much can be harmful. People with liver conditions should use it cautiously and consult a doctor

Side Effects

Most people do not experience side effects, but rare cases include:

  • Allergic reactions
  • Skin rash
  • Nausea

Seek medical help if unusual symptoms appear.

Why It’s Popular

Paracetamol is widely used because it is effective, affordable, and safe for most age groups. It is available in many forms — tablets, syrups, dissolvable powders, and injections — making it easy to use in different situations.

Conclusion

Paracetamol remains one of the most trusted medicines for quick relief from pain and fever. When taken responsibly, it is a powerful and safe tool to improve comfort and well-being.
